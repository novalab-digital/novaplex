About Us

A studio shaped by craft, vision and detail.

Founded in 2022, redefining modern spaces in the UAE.

NovaPlex is a dynamic, creative architecture and interior design studio with a vision to redefine modern living through innovative design, functionality and timeless aesthetics. We've grown into a trusted partner for residential, commercial and hospitality projects — delivering tailored solutions that reflect each client's identity and aspirations.

Our team of architects, engineers and project managers creates environments that blend creativity, comfort and practicality. From luxurious residences to refined commercial spaces, every project carries our commitment to quality, attention to detail and design excellence.
